About Drauger OS
What is Drauger OS?
Drauger OS is a Desktop Linux Gaming Operating System.
Our aim is to provide a platform for gamers to get great performance without sacrificing security. From Keyboard and Mouse to a Full Flight Simulator, our goal is to make it easy for anyone to start playing their favorite games quickly.
Since Drauger OS is based on Ubuntu LTS, it's secure, stable and will continue to recieves regular updates even if your version of Drauger OS isn't currently supported by our Developers.
What Drauger OS is NOT
Drauger OS is NOT for everyday use.
For this reason, it does not come with some apps that you may find "common". Although they will still function, Drauger OS does not come with audio or video editing software (i.e. Audacity and Kdenlive) nor an Office Suite (i.e. LibreOffice).

Furthermore, Drauger OS is NOT based on: Arch, Fedora, or Solus.
There are no plans to rebase Drauger OS to another distro for various reasons. Most impotantly, the majority of other distributions would require copious amounts of work to get to the point we are at now.
This is not to say they are not good distributions, but rather they do not fit our vision of where to take Drauger OS and do not fit our development process.
The Drauger OS vision of the Future
Drauger OS was created for several reasons.
The primary of which is that other gaming distributions were either abandoned, difficult to use, or did not have a strong community supporting them. To avoid the issues listed above, we observe the following tenets when developing Drauger OS:

Continuity: We have structured Drauger OS Development around a team concept and distributed leadership. That way, the project won't stop if a key member discontinues work.

Ease of Use: We aim to make Drauger OS user-friendly. Specifically, we try to make tasks intuitive while also ensuring that software and hardware "just works".

Community Growth: We always welcome community engagement! The more we hear what people want, the better we can do. If you want to voice your opinion, check out our Telegram Group, Discord Server or subreddit!

Make Support Easy: We work hard to make sure there are a number of ways anyone can contact the Drauger OS Devs. The easiest ways are through the same methods listed in "Grow the Community" above.
Development & media
Drauger OS is a free and open-source, community driven project. If you wish to participate in development, contribute code, update documentation, help users, or provide feedback, feel free to contact us in any of the listed ways, and check out our “Contributing” page for info on how to give back. If you wish to learn more about Drauger OS, or contact the people behind it, feel free to contact us.
Contributing
With Drauger OS being free and Open-Source, we accept contributions of all sorts: From code and documentation, to donations and goods, if it will aid in development it's welcome.

If you wish to contribute to Drauger OS, please reach out to us. If you meet certain milestones, you may be listed on our contributors page.
Right now, we especially need help with:
Code: Mostly Python and BASH, but also growing amounts of C++ and CSS.
Documentation: Man pages, README files, Change Logs, you name it.

If you want to contribute and have no programming experience, please consider handling documentation or language translations. All that is required is basic English literacy (and in the case of translations, literacy in another language).
If you are still unsure, consider donating or sending us testing equipment off our Amazon Wishlist
